NIKE, “I, DAVID”

“Featuring famed ballet dancer David Hallberg, ‘I, David’ explores the notion of perseverance. Perseverance both literally - through Hallberg’s personal experience in overcoming physical injury - and metaphorically - using the NikeLab ACG Poncho as an allegory for tenacity, for pushing forward in the face of harsh conditions.”

-Nike
